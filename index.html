About Us



Era Homes (Pvt) Ltd is proud to build the very best ‘Space for life’ with the ethical and quality constructions with the supervision of professionals of Chartered Architects, Chartered Structural Engineers, Project Managers and well experienced technical team to bring your dream home to success.

All our works are carried out to the highest quality standard with the branded materials for customer satisfaction, happiness and for their healthy through the space living environment. Therefore, durability of our constructions are guaranteed and committed.

We strive to build aesthetically pleasing houses which retain the true feel of family living. Our all constructions are followed the Sri Lankan traditional customs procedures via that those industry professionals from very beginning to end of constructions. We always believe that a well-built home creates happiness, good health, and prosperity.

Our aim is always to create great space for life, therefore our products are not just the constructions, more than that gives feel to relax and freedom through the space.
